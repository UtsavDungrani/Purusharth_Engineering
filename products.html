Slip-On Flanges

High-quality MS slip-on flanges designed for easy installation and reliable performance.

Our slip-on flanges feature a smooth bore that allows the pipe to slide through the flange. They are welded both inside and outside to provide sufficient strength and prevent leakage. Perfect for low-pressure applications and easy maintenance.

  • Easy installation and alignment
  • Cost-effective solution
  • Available in various sizes
  • CNC finished for precision
  • Auto-drilled bolt holes

Blind Flanges

Reliable MS blind flanges for sealing pipe ends and pressure testing applications.

Blind flanges are solid discs used to seal off the ends of piping systems and pressure vessel openings. They are commonly used for pressure testing and when future expansion is planned. Our blind flanges are manufactured to exact specifications.

  • Excellent sealing capability
  • High pressure resistance
  • Easy to install and remove
  • Available in all standard sizes
  • Precision CNC machining
Get Quote

Bend Spooling

Custom pipe bending and spooling solutions for complex piping systems.

Our bend spooling services provide custom pipe bending solutions for industrial applications. We use advanced machinery to create precise bends while maintaining the structural integrity of the pipes. Perfect for complex piping layouts.

  • Custom bending solutions
  • Precise angle control
  • Maintains pipe integrity
  • Reduces welding joints
  • Cost-effective installation
Get Quote

Threaded Flanges

MS threaded flanges for easy assembly and disassembly without welding.

Threaded flanges are designed with internal threads that match the external threads on the pipe. This allows for easy assembly and disassembly without welding, making them ideal for maintenance and repair applications.

  • No welding required
  • Easy assembly/disassembly
  • Ideal for maintenance
  • Available in various thread sizes
  • High-quality threading
Get Quote

Multi PCD Flanges

Versatile MS flanges with multiple Pitch Circle Diameters (PCD) for flexible installation.

Multi PCD flanges are designed to accommodate various bolt patterns, making them ideal for applications where compatibility with different standards is required. These flanges offer flexibility and ease of use in diverse piping systems.

  • Multiple bolt patterns for universal fit
  • Reduces inventory requirements
  • Precision CNC manufactured
  • Available in a range of sizes
  • Durable and reliable construction
